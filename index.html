Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything clients and recruiters ask before reaching out — answered honestly.

How much do you charge for a project?▾
Pricing depends on scope - from small landing pages to full-stack AI-integrated apps. Email me your project details for a custom quote. I always provide a fixed price upfront - no hourly surprises.
How long does a project take?▾
Landing pages: 3–7 days. Full-stack web apps: 2–5 weeks. AI-integrated platforms: 4–8 weeks. Timelines are fixed and agreed upon before work begins. I work in 1–2 week sprints with demos after each sprint so you always see progress.
How do we communicate during the project?▾
Primarily Email or Slack - whichever you prefer. I share daily progress updates, weekly video demos via Loom, and am responsive within 2–4 hours during working hours (IST). You'll never wonder what's happening.
Do I own the code? What about IP?▾
100% yes — you own every line of code I write for you. I provide full GitHub repository access, and upon final payment, all intellectual property transfers to you. No lock-in, no licensing fees, no strings attached.
What if I need changes after the project is done?▾
All projects include 30 days of free post-launch support for bug fixes. After that, I offer maintenance retainers starting at ₹3,000/month. I also provide full documentation so you can work with any developer later.
Can you work with my existing codebase?▾
Absolutely — I've done this at PropgrowthX where I joined an existing MERN codebase mid-product and shipped new modules. I'll do a code review first, give you an honest assessment, then quote accordingly. No "I need to rewrite everything" nonsense.
What's your primary tech stack?▾
Primary: React/Next.js + Node.js/Express + MongoDB/PostgreSQL + AWS/Azure. Also comfortable with Flask, Django, Firebase, GraphQL, React Native, and most common web technologies. If you have a specific requirement, just ask — I'll be honest about my familiarity.
Are you available for full-time roles?▾
Yes! Actively looking for full-time SWE, AI Engineer, or Full Stack roles — remote or hybrid. Target: product-led startups and AI-first companies. Email udayapril22@gmail.com or connect on LinkedIn.
